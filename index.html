National Academic Innovation Hub

Organizing and showcasing student projects from across India
Details

Empowering students through knowledge sharing

The National Academic Innovation Hub (NAIH) is an integrated platform designed to bring together student projects from universities and colleges across India. With regional and college-wise segmentation, user-friendly interface, and a peer review mechanism, NAIH enables students to showcase their work, learn from peers, and foster cross-functional research.
features

Key Features

Discover the powerful features of the National Academic Innovation Hub (NAIH)

Regional and College-wise Segmentation

Categorize student projects based on regions and further divide them by colleges within each region.

User-Friendly Interface

Create an intuitive and user-friendly interface for easy navigation of projects based on region and college.

Institutional Collaboration

Collaborate with universities and colleges nationwide to encourage sharing of project information. Standardized templates for project documentation will ensure uniform data entry.

Peer Review Mechanism

Integrate a peer review system for students and faculty members to provide constructive feedback and suggestions on projects within their region and affiliated colleges.

FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is the National Academic Innovation Hub (NAIH)? The National Academic Innovation Hub (NAIH) is an online platform designed to organize and showcase student projects from technical and higher educational institutes across India.
What is the objective of the NAIH? The objective of the NAIH is to develop an integrated platform where universities and colleges can provide information about the projects done by their students, promoting peer learning and cross-functional research.
How will the projects be categorized on the NAIH? The projects will be categorized based on regions and further divided by colleges within each region.
What features will the NAIH offer for users to navigate projects? The NAIH will have a user-friendly interface that allows users to navigate projects based on region and college.
How will universities and colleges collaborate with the NAIH? Universities and colleges will be encouraged to share information about the projects undertaken by their students. Standardized templates for project documentation will facilitate uniform data entry.
